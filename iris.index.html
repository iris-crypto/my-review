🔄 Change

今年最核心的变化是角色定位的升级：从单一模块的执行者，成长为能够独立负责一个国家市场、横跨 ASO - 产品 - 合规 - 支付 - 推送 - UA 投放多条业务线的综合操盘手。 The most significant shift this year was a fundamental role upgrade: from a single-module executor to an independent operator spanning ASO, product, compliance, payments, push notifications, and UA — owning an entire country market end-to-end.

📢 UA
第一次踏入付费增长领域First Steps into Paid Acquisition

UA 投放是今年全新接触的工作领域。此前的工作集中在有机增长（ASO），而 UA 意味着要直接面对预算管理、素材创意、平台算法和实时数据决策——每一个环节都是从零开始。 UA was an entirely new domain this year. Previously focused on organic growth (ASO), UA meant directly facing budget management, creative production, platform algorithms, and real-time data-driven decisions — every aspect started from zero.

📚

投放基础体系学习Learning the UA Fundamentals

从零学习 TikTok Ads 和 Meta Ads 平台的投放规则、账户结构、预算分配逻辑和素材审核标准，构建完整的投放认知框架。Learned TikTok Ads and Meta Ads platform rules, account structures, budget allocation logic, and creative review standards from scratch, building a complete UA cognitive framework.

这是一次从"完全不了解"到"能够独立操作"的学习过程。系统性地掌握了两个主要投放平台（TikTok、Meta）的操作逻辑：如何搭建账户结构、如何设置广告系列与广告组、如何理解平台的出价和竞价机制、如何根据不同目标（安装、付费、留存）选择合适的投放策略。同时学习了各平台对素材格式、时长、内容的审核要求，避免因素材不合规导致的投放中断。This was a learning journey from "completely unfamiliar" to "independently operational." Systematically mastered the operational logic of two major ad platforms (TikTok, Meta): account structure setup, campaign/ad set configuration, bidding and auction mechanism understanding, and selecting appropriate strategies for different objectives (installs, purchases, retention). Also learned each platform's creative format, duration, and content review requirements to avoid campaign interruptions from non-compliant assets.

I progressively built sensitivity to key metrics: whether CPI is within reasonable range, whether CTR indicates sufficient creative appeal, whether low CVR points to creative or landing page issues, and whether ROAS supports continued spending. Capable of tiering creatives by performance (scale up / observe / shut down), developing preliminary data-centric campaign optimization decision-making capabilities.

AI 的核心价值不在于替代人，而在于让一个人能承担原本需要多个人才能完成的工作量。面对任何重复性高、规则明确的工作，第一反应不再是"怎么做"，而是"能不能让 AI 做"。 AI's core value isn't about replacing people — it's about enabling one person to handle workloads that previously required an entire team. For any repetitive, rule-based task, my first instinct is no longer "how do I do this?" but "can AI do this?"

🔍 优化空间与后续思考🔍 Areas for Improvement & Reflections

  • 当前 AI 应用集中在"执行层提效"，下一步需探索 AI 在"决策层辅助"方面的可能性，如素材方向预测、产品功能优先级建议等。Current AI applications focus on "execution-level efficiency." Next: explore AI's potential in "decision-level assistance" — e.g., creative direction prediction, feature priority recommendations.
  • UA 自动化流程需注意稳定性和容错机制。自动化出错的代价往往高于人工操作，必须加入异常检测和人工确认节点。The UA automation pipeline must prioritize stability and error-handling. Automation errors carry higher costs than manual mistakes — anomaly detection and human confirmation checkpoints are essential.
  • AI 工具的选择上，目前更多是"遇到问题找工具"的模式，尚未形成系统性认知。后续应定期做工具盘点和效果评估。AI tool selection is currently reactive. A systematic understanding of the AI tool ecosystem is needed, with regular audits and effectiveness evaluations.
